Summer Fun :)


Northwestern Shadow Day

Through ETHS (Evanston Township Highschool) and Northwestern's Partnership, myself and other ETHS stem students were able to shadow STEM graduate students at Northwestern. We were to choose what fields we were interested in and being interested in chemical engineering I chose to shadow a ChemE major. My gradudate student's name was Supna and she walked me through the research that she is doing, we did a Lab where we made a battery from scratch and she explained how creating these batteries would help store energy in a more environmentally friendly way. It was a very meaningful experience for me and it opened my eyes to all the diiferent types of things I could do in the future.
